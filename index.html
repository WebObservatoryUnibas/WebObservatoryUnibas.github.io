Data Sets

We provide extracted data sets from Web observations in machine readable means e.g. JSON. These data sets may be used for further analysis or visualisations in order to give a deeper understanding.

Case Studies

The abundance of Web data is a big challenge and makes it rather difficult to mine, tranform, analyse, compare and interpret useful information in a meaningful way. Our projects aim to understand activities on the Web through research, analysis and visualisation in real-time.

Data Sets

Following Data Sets are available on our GitHub repository:

  • Price observation of 6 Easyjet Flights in December 2015 from Basel (BSL) to London-Gatwick (LGW) and return over a period of 7 months:
    https://github.com/WebObservatoryUnibas/lab/blob/master/easyjet_BSL-LGW.json
  • Tram delay observation at station "Barfüsserplatz" over a period of one month:
    https://github.com/WebObservatoryUnibas/lab/blob/master/TramDelayTime.json
  • Observation of comments on the news portal "20min.ch", tree based:
    https://github.com/WebObservatoryUnibas/lab/blob/master/2015_06_comments20min_treebased.json.zip
