Data Sets

We provide extracted data sets from Web observations in machine readable means e.g. JSON. These data sets may be used for further analysis or visualisations in order to give a deeper understanding.

Research Projects

The abundance of Web data is a big challenge and makes it rather difficult to mine, tranform, analyse, compare and interpret useful information in a meaningful way. Real-Time Web observation projects make data sets available for the general public. Our projects aim to understand activities on the Web through research, analysis and visualisation.
